Epidemiologist, Researcher, Community Organizer 

Jason is an applied epidemiologist wih over six years of experience in government public health and an on-going four year as an epidemiologist. He brings expertise in infectious disease, health equity, epidemiology surveillance, and community assessments. Informed by his identity as a Filipino American, Catholic social teaching, and community, he stands by his work that strives for a healthier and opportune future for all. 

Outside his days of reading articles and policies, coding, and Teams messages, you can find him trying not to throw gutter balls at the bowling alley, running errands on a bike, writing poetry, drinking boba, taking public transit, and officiating as a USATF/CTSA track and field official.
